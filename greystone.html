In November of 2016, I participated in NaNoWriMo, an event where thousands of people try to write an entire novel in one month. Due to having recently finished The Wheel of Time series (which was fantastic, by the way), my planned novel was of the "epic fantasy" genre. Note that at this point, I've finished perhaps two or three short stories.

After two weeks of writing, I gave up. The story was chock-full of ridiculous plot points, flat characters, unclear motivations (not in a good way), and hundreds upon hundreds of Strunk&White violations. If you're dead-set on reading it, here's a compiled PDF of the story's corpse:
